How it works

One Ecosystem.
Three Roles.

TRIBE26 connects communities through a layered participation model — every person, business, and location plays a distinct role in building a stronger local network.

Member
Consume & Connect

Join the TRIBE26 community. Discover local businesses, participate in events, and experience Greater Vancouver through a network built for you.

Contributor
Create & Earn

Validate your skills and services within the ecosystem. Reach an engaged local audience and grow your presence across the network.

Node / Anchor
Sustain & Infrastructure

Physical and digital locations that power the network. Nodes provide sustained infrastructure — the backbone of the TRIBE26 ecosystem across every neighbourhood.
